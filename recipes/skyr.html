Skyr

Thick and Creamy Icelandic Yogurt

Ingredients

  • 6 cups skim milk
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Rennet
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Steps:

  1. Heat the milk to 185-195째F (85-90째C) and hold it at that temperature for about 10 minutes. Be careful not to boil or scorch the milk. Cool down to 100-102째F (38-39째C). It is important that you allow the milk to cool properly, or else the rennet may not work.

  2. Stir the sour cream into a tablespoon of milk until well mixed. Pour into the warm milk and mix well. Add the rennet.

  3. Allow the skyr to cool about 6 hours. You will be ready to proceed to the next step when you can make a cut in the skyr which will not close immediately.

  4. Line a sieve or colander with cheesecloth or a fine linen cloth and pour in the skyr. Tie the ends of the cloth together over the top and hang over a bucket or other container so the whey can drip off.

  5. Allow the skyr to drain until it is fairly firm. The consistency should be like ice cream.
