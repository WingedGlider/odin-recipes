Viking Bread

Simple Bread Made from Oats and Barley

Ingredients

  • 900 grams of a mixture of wheat, barley, oat, and rye flour
  • 3 cups of buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 dash of salt
  • ½ cup of honey
  • 1 cup of chopped nuts

Steps:

  1. Mix and knead the ingredients thoroughly. Use an electric mixer with a dough hook to knead the dough, as the ingredients are dense, making it hard to mix by hand.

  2. Using your hands covered with flour, form the dough into small balls, about 2-3 inches in diameter (5-8 cm).

  3. To bake, press the balls flat, about ½ inch thick (1 cm), on a flat pan greased with butter over the fire. Turn the bread once to cook on both sides.

  4. When done, the bread is light brown and sounds hollow when tapped, about 2-3 minutes on each side. Eat warm.
